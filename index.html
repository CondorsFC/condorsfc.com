About Condors FC

Founded in August 2023, Condors FC is a competitive men's soccer club in Kelowna, BC. Built on community, culture, and consistency, we compete in the KMSL and OPL leagues with a long-term vision of becoming one of BC’s top adult soccer programs.

We’ve earned podium finishes across multiple leagues and are now focused on growing our men's program through disciplined training, strategic media growth, and financial sustainability.
