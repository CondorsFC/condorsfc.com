About Condors FC

Founded in August 2023, Condors FC is a premiere adult-level competitive soccer club based in Kelowna, British Columbia.

Though the city has long been home to various youth soccer programs, few options exist for adult players seeking structured, high-level competition. From the beginning, Condors FC was built not only to compete, but to foster a culture rooted in community, identity, and continuous growth. The club blends passion with talent, and seeks to be something that players are proud to represent, and their friends and families are excited to follow and support.

In just 2 years, Condors FC has fielded multiple Co-Ed and Men’s teams across the Pacific Adult Soccer League, Urban Rec, the Kelowna Men’s Soccer League, and the Okanagan Premier League, earning 1st, 2nd and 3rd place finishes along the way. Moving forward into 2026, we have made the strategic decision to consolidate the club’s energy and resources into developing the Men’s program, placing the Co-Ed and potential Women’s teams on hold for the time being. What started as a bold idea continues to evolve into a structured and motivated club driven by the hard work and commitment of the players and managers. We continue to be excited to see what more we will continue to achieve in the years ahead!
